Submission Guidelines

Papers presented at the WACV workshops will be published as part of the "WACV Workshops Proceedings" and should, therefore, follow the same presentation guideliness as the main conference. Workshop papers will be included in IEEE Xplore, but will be indexed separatelly from the main conference papers.

The Author Toolkit (latex only) is available both on Overleaf and in Github. The submission website can be found here (CMT).
